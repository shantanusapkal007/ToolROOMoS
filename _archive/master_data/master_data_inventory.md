# Master Data Inventory

This document tracks the required master data tables for ToolRoomOS. All entries here will need to be populated during onboarding or via the `imports/` mechanism.

## Required Master Data
- `Material Grades`: Standardized list of steel, aluminum, and other raw material grades.
- `Machine List`: Inventory of all CNC, VMC, Lathe, and Assembly machines with hourly rates.
- `Departments`: Production, Quality, Maintenance, Dispatch, Engineering.
- `Operations`: Turning, Milling, Grinding, Drilling, Heat Treatment, Surface Finish.
- `Units`: Kgs, Nos, Liters, Meters, Sets.
- `Vendors`: Approved suppliers for raw materials and outside processing.
- `Customers`: Client directory with shipping and billing addresses.
- `Employees`: Machine operators, quality inspectors, engineers.
- `Roles`: System access roles mapped to the workflows.
- `Surface Finish`: Standardized finish requirements.
- `Tolerance`: Standard machining tolerances.
- `Heat Treatment`: Types of heat treatment applied.
- `Currencies`: USD, EUR, INR, etc.
