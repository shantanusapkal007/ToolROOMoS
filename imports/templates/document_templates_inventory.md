# Document Templates Inventory

This document tracks the required import templates and mapping configurations for ToolRoomOS's document intelligence engine.

## Required Templates
- `excel/`: Templates for bulk master data uploads (e.g. BOM imports).
- `pdf/`: Schemas for parsing unstructured PDF orders.
- `drawings/`: Guidelines for OCR and dimension extraction from engineering drawings.
- `customer_po/`: Field mappings for extracting Customer Purchase Orders.
- `vendor_po/`: Templates for generating Vendor Purchase Orders.
- `grn/`: Field mappings for supplier Goods Receipt Notes.
- `reports/`: Templates for Machine Shop Daily Reports and Inspection Reports.
